📘 Basic Hand Signs

👋 HI
Raise open palm and wave slowly.
🙏 THANK YOU
Touch fingers near chin and move outward.
🆘 HELP
Thumb up with closed fist.
👍 YES
Show thumbs up.
👎 NO
Show thumbs down.
💧 WATER
Three fingers near mouth.
✋ STOP
Open palm facing camera.
🍚 FOOD
Bring fingers toward mouth.
🏥 HOSPITAL
Cross index fingers in X shape.
🌞 GOOD MORNING
Open palm rising upward.
